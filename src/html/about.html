About Craftilo Tiles
Craftilo Tiles began its journey in 2014 with a clear vision: to revolutionize the tiling industry by providing exceptional products that are both beautiful and durable. Starting as a small company with a passion for design and craftsmanship, Craftilo rapidly gained recognition for its meticulous attention to detail and superior tile-making processes. Over the years, the brand has grown, diversifying its product portfolio to cater to both modern and traditional tastes. Whether it's sleek, minimalist tiles for contemporary homes or intricate, hand-crafted designs for more classic spaces, Craftilo has continued to innovate, staying ahead of design trends while maintaining timeless craftsmanship. Today, the company is recognized globally, with clients spanning residential homes, luxury apartments, and commercial buildings.

Our Story
Craftilo Tiles’ mission is to blend artistry with functionality in every tile they create. Every piece is designed with careful thought, focusing on elevating spaces into expressions of personal style and comfort. Sustainability is at the heart of Craftilo's manufacturing process. The company is committed to using eco-friendly materials and sustainable manufacturing techniques that reduce waste and minimize environmental impact. By investing in innovation, Craftilo consistently pushes the boundaries of what’s possible in tile design, introducing unique textures, finishes, and patterns that can stand the test of time. With a focus on both aesthetics and durability, each tile from Craftilo serves as a functional work of art, capable of transforming any space.

Our Mission
Craftilo Tiles' commitment to sustainability, innovation, and quality forms the core of its business ethos. Sustainability efforts focus on responsibly sourcing raw materials and employing eco-friendly production techniques, ensuring the company contributes positively to the environment. Craftilo also places a strong emphasis on innovation, continually experimenting with new tile materials, finishes, and design methods to keep up with changing interior design trends. Whether creating tiles that mimic the look of natural stone or developing ultra-modern finishes that offer a sleek, reflective surface, Craftilo ensures that quality never takes a backseat. Every tile undergoes rigorous quality testing to ensure durability, ensuring that customers get the best possible product for their spaces.
